INTRO

I am Hritwik, I like to work with data. My ideal is all available data, represents a problem that can to be solved for the sake of acquiring the intelligence concealed in that data, and solving this problem is the responsibility of a data analyst. You can observe this ideal reflected in my work.

My approach to data analysis is rooted in a strong understanding of business needs and goals. I use tools like Python, SQL, and PowerBI to collect and analyze data from a variety of sources, and I'm skilled in creating customized visualizations that communicate complex insights in a clear and compelling way. As a data analyst, I'm always seeking new challenges and opportunities to apply my skills in innovative ways. I'm particularly interested in projects that involve working collaboratively with cross-functional teams to solve complex business problems.

If you believe your assignment calls for such an analyst, feel free to get in touch with me. I look forward to hearing from you.
